Flat Bar Insulator

The flat bar insulator is used with the flat bar profile posts to hold the wire on inner post or hook with a tensioner. The flat bar insulators have the following features:

  • Quick fit clips on the top and bottom for faster installation
  • Feed through clip option built in
  • Easy to change and maintain
  • Available in black

Large Tail Hook

The large tail hooks are a quick fit hook system for the Nemtek flat  bar insulators  to connect tensioners. They offer the following features:

  • Stainless steel finish for long life expectancy
  • Have a large tail to fit the Nemtek range of tensioning devices
  • Fit quickly onto the Nemtek round and flat bar insulators.

Effective solutions across many sectors

Fence protection systems with intruder detection are the first layer of the perimeter protection. The fence mounted detectors can sense climbing, cutting, sawing of the fence, which means we can detect any penetration determination, before the intruder could enter into the protected area, provided enough time to the guards to react.

This type of systems can be used for perimeter protection of solar power plants, prisons, logistic centers, airports, power plants, refineries, military objects, but also in the residential areas to keep you and your family safe.

As a leader in security systems, we can install effective electrical fencing solutions for your premises.
